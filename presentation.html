About me

I am Jean-Luc GOUTHON, a PhD student in Statistics/Data Science with a strong passion for deep learning methods for time series and statistical/geostatistical modeling. My research focuses on multi-population mortality modeling, combining deep learning techniques with geostatistical and spatio-temporal clustering methods to capture dependencies between populations and mortality-related variables.

With a solid background in mathematics and Python/R programming, I leverage these skills to tackle complex problems in my field.

  • Time Series Modeling & Forecasting

  • Data Visualization & Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Academic Research & Scientific Communication
