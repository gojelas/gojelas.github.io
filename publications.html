Impact of above-average heat on mortality: extension of longevity models to account for global warming

An extension of Seklecka et al. (2017), which specifically models the impact of above-average heat. Our approach complements existing longevity models, demonstrates better robustness indicators, and can forecast future mortality based on various climate scenarios.
